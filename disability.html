Disability Services

Creating Community Impact also offers more intensive services for Individuals who experience Intellectual/Developmental Disabilities. Our company wants to ensure that there are no barriers on the path to employment and any person can find employment in any vocation he or she is passionate about. They are Discovery, Employment Path, and Long Term Job Supports.

Discovery:

What is Discovery?
The purpose is to help individuals learn about their unique skills and talents or in other words, “Your personal genius”. Creating Community Impact uses a Person Centered approach spending one to one time with people at their home and in the community. They help people identify their likes/dislikes.

Employment Path:

What is Employment Path?
Employment Path Services are services to provide learning and work experiences. It may including volunteer opportunities.

Long Term Job Supports:

Creating Community Impact provides job coaching support to individuals who are in community jobs. Creating community Impact job coaches are there to support people in becoming as independent as possible at their job.
